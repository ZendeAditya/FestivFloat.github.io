welcome to FestivalFloat

Balloon Decorating Company is a family owned business located in uk. We have been providing quality balloon decorations since 2004. Our team of professional designers create custom balloons for any occasion. Whether you need to celebrate a birthday party, wedding, graduation, or just want to make a statement at your office holiday party, we can help! We offer free consultations and design services to ensure that you get exactly what you want. We specialize in helium filled balloons, latex balloons, and sugar coated balloons. We also provide custom designs and corporate branding. Balloon Decorating Company offers a wide variety of balloon shapes including hearts, stars, flowers, animals, and much more. We use only high-quality materials and strive to exceed our customers' expectations. If you would like to learn more about us, please contact us today!
